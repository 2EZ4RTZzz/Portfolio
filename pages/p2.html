Game story:

Howard is an adventurer who has been invited to visit a famous businessman's castle in a small town. However, the residents' words and the castle's rules seem to make the castle more secret and creepy. During the exploration inside the castle, Howard finally knows that the businessman is the devil who set the trap to hunt humans, he has to defeat the businessman to get out of the castle.

Core Mechanics:

For the PC: Arrow key up down left right, space bar for the behavior function key.(for example, open gate/open box, chat with NPC, interactive function),Esc for calling the game setting page.

For multimedia devices: Tap on the screen to allow the character to move to the click location. Double tapping will activate the behavioral function. The top-left corner of the screen will pop up with information about the game's settings

About game ending

This is a multi-linear game, each decision the player makes in the game may lead the game to a different ending.

Gameplay features:

Challenges within the game include solving puzzles, fighting, and avoiding obstacles. Players get hints and answer questions by talking to NPC' s in order to solve puzzles. Complete the puzzle to get key props such as gems. When encountering monsters to fight against, use weapons and skills to defeat enemies and obtain props. Defeat the monsters to get exp and props. In some maps there will be shadows and obstacles, players move to avoid them.

Player description:

The game player is suitable for players of non-specific gender over 16 years old. The game is intended for Casual type players to experience the immersive play experience brought by the RPG game, controlling the main character to adventure and complete the challenges.
